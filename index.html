TipIt now available for Discordapp.

Quickly and Easily Send Money!

Tipit.io allows a quick and easy way to send money to other people within popular platforms like Discord, Twitter, text, email, FaceBook, and others.

Tipit uses very simple commands to check balance, send money, send to everyone in group, and others.

Commands on Discordapp:

  • First invite @TipIt-TipBot to the Discord group.
  • Send EOS tokens to tipitaccount with your Discord name and number.
    =>For example EOS Memo: yourdiscord#1234
  • !bal
    =>Shows balance of user who runs !bal
  • !send @DiscordUser 0.1 EOS
    =>Sends amount of 0.1 EOS to DiscordUser from the person running the command.
  • !withdraw EOSaccount 1.0 EOS
    =>Withdraws 1.0 EOS to the EOSaccount
    This is the only time an EOS account is required.
    TipIt only charges on withdraws for just 1% fee.
